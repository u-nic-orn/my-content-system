## Content Guidelines
<!-- Specific tone, style, or format rules discovered during work -->
- **it-CH formal possessives:** Always capitalise Lei-form possessives — "il Suo", "i Suoi", "la Sua" (not "il suo")
- **it-CH greetings:** Use "Salve" not "Ciao" when body copy is in formal Lei register
- **it-CH buttons:** Glossary uses informal imperative (tu) or infinitive for button labels — this is acceptable convention even when body text uses formal Lei
- **it-CH AI term:** "AI" → "l'IA" in running text; full form "IA di bexio" per glossary
- **fr-CH pending status:** "en suspens" (not "en attente") — per master-glossary.csv
- **fr-CH paid status:** "payée" — no "déjà"; bare past participle only
